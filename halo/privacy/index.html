Halo Privacy Policy

Effective Date: 1 August 2022

Introduction

This privacy policy (“Privacy Policy”) applies to all users of the Halo mobile app (“App”), which is offered by Stijn Heymans (“Halo”, “we”, “us”). Please read this Privacy Policy carefully. By accessing or using any part of the “App”, you acknowledge you have been informed of and consent to our practices with regard to your personal information and data.

Data Storing

Halo does not store any of your data.

Network Access

Halo app DOES NOT track you, DOES NOT collect your data and DOES NOT send your data to remote servers, with the following exception:
  1. When you scan an ISBN of a book, we fetch book information for that ISBN from the Open Library. We never store that ISBN.

Device Access

In order to scan ISBNs on books, Halo will request camera access.

Contacting Halo about your privacy

If you have questions or concerns about the way we are handling your information, or would like to exercise your privacy rights, please send an email to stijn.heymans@gmail.com. We will respond within 10 days of receiving your message.

Privacy policy changes

Although most changes are likely to be minor, Halo may change its privacy policy from time to time, and in Halo's sole discretion. our continued use of the app after any change in this Privacy Policy will constitute your acceptance of such change.
